Bree Street & De Waterkant

Morning cortados at Origin Coffee, pastel de nata from Jason Bakery, and sunset cocktails on The Silo rooftop. The cobbled lanes of De Waterkant glow pastel at dusk.

Muizenberg & Kalk Bay

Surf gentle rollers, hunt vintage vinyl at Kalk Bay Books, then choose snoek tacos right on the harbor. Finish with soft serve from Ice Café.

Constantia Winelands

Cycle between estates, sample cool-climate chardonnay, and book a long-table lunch under ancient oaks at Groot Constantia.
